Recipe #1

Hearty Winter soup
  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Pre cooked chicken 400g
Vegetable oil 1 tbsp
Carrots 3
Celery stalks 2
Onion 1
Water 300ml
Chicken stock 1300ml
Double cream 180ml
Chicken stock 1300ml
Cannellini beans 400g

Step 1: Heat the oil in a large pot, over a medium heat.

Step 2: Saut√© carrots, celery, onion, and heat for 8-10 minutes until vegetables are soft.

Step 3: Add water, chicken and stock and bring to boil, whilst stirring.

Step 4: Reduce heat and simmer fro 15-20 minutes.

Step 5: Add beans and cook for a further 5 minutes.

Step 6: If too thick, add more stock or water.

Step 7: Season.

Recipe #3

Apricot Yoghurt Fool
  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Yoghurt, greek 1000g
Apricots, canned in syrup 900g
Honey 180g
Cinnamon 1 tbsp
Caster sugar 100g

Step 1: Place the apricots, sugar and cinnamon in a pan, cover and cook gently for about 15 mins, until the apricots are very soft, set aside to cool.

Step 2: Spoon the yoghurt into a bowl and fold in most of the cooled apricots.

Step 3: Spoon into serving glasses and top with the remaining fruit and any juices.
